I am currently a prospective Economics PhD student. My research interests lie in the intersection of industrial organization and information economics, using tools from game theory and mechanism design to study strategic behavior in markets with asymmetric information, with an interest in both theoretical and applied microeconomic approaches. As I continue to develop as a researcher, I remain open to exploring a broad range of topics within economics.
